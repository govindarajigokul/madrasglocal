Commodity Trading and Export

Madras Glocal Food specializes in the trading and export of high-quality agri and food commodities, connecting Indian producers directly to global markets. By sourcing directly from farmers and aggregators, we ensure better pricing, traceability, and consistent quality.

We handle end-to-end trading and export management, including sourcing, quality checks, packaging, documentation, and logistics. Our streamlined process ensures that products reach international buyers efficiently and reliably.

We work with a wide range of commodities, including grains, pulses, spices, oil seeds, and traditional food products, catering to the needs of global buyers, importers, and food brands.

With Madras Glocal Food, you gain a trusted partner for building scalable, transparent, and efficient supply chains from village producers to global markets.
